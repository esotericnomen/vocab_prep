early 15c., restyffe not moving forward, from Middle French restif motionless, brought to a
standstill (Modern French r&#233;tif), from rester to remain (see <a
href=/index.php?term=rest&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>rest</a> (n.2)). Sense of
unmanageable (1680s) evolved via notion of a horse refusing to go forward.
