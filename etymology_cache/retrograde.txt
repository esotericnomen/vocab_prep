late 14c., originally of the apparent motions of planets, from Latin retrogradus going back, going
backward, from retrogradi move backward, from retro- backward (see <a
href=/index.php?term=retro-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>retro-</a>) + gradi to go,
