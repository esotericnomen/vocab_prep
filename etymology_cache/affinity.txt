from Latin affinitatem (nominative affinitas) neighborhood, relationship by marriage, noun of state
from affinis adjacent, also kin by marriage, literally bordering on, from ad- to (see <a

end (see <a href=/index.php?term=finish&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>finish</a>
