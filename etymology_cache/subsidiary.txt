1540s, from Latin subsidiarius belonging to a reserve, of a reserve, reserved; serving to assist or
supplement, from subsidium a help, aid, relief, troops in reserve (see <a
href=/index.php?term=subsidy&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>subsidy</a>). As a noun,
c.1600, subsidiary thing. In Latin the word was used as a noun meaning the reserve.
