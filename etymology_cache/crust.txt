1814, Modern Latin neuter plural of crustaceus (animalia), literally having a crust or shell, from
Latin crusta crust, rind, bark, hard shell (see <a href=/index.php?term=crust&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>crust</a> (n.)). Taken as a zoological classification by Lamarck, 1801; Cuvier
(1798) had les insectes crustac&eacute;es.
