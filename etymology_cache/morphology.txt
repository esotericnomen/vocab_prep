morphology (n.)

1824 in biology (from German Morphologie, 1817); 1869 in philology; from morpho- + -logy. Related:
Morphological; morphologist. Related: Morphologist.


geomorphology (n.)

1893, from geo- + morphology. Form geomorphy is from 1889. Related: Geomorphological;
geomorphologically; geomorphologist.
