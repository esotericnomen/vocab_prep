early 15c., repeat again and again, from Late Latin reiteratus, past participle of reiterare to
repeat, from re- again (see <a href=/index.php?term=re-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>re-</a>) + iterare to repeat, from iterum again (see <a
href=/index.php?term=iteration&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>iteration</a>). Related:
Reiterated; reiterating.
