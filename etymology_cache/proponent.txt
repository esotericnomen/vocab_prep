1580s, one who brings forth a proposition or argument, from Latin proponentem (nominative
proponens), present participle of proponere put forward (see <a
href=/index.php?term=propound&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>propound</a>). In part
also a native formation from <a href=/index.php?term=propone&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>propone</a>. As an adjective from 1680s.
