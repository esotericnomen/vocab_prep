mid-14c., ascrive, from Old French ascrivre to inscribe; attribute, impute, from Latin ascribere to
write in, to add to in a writing, from ad- to (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ad-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ad-</a>) + scribere to write
(see <a href=/index.php?term=script&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>script</a> (n.)).
Spelling restored by 16c. Related: Ascribed; ascribing.
