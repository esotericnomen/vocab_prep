1650s, from Greek tetralogia, from tetra- (see <a href=/index.php?term=tetra-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>tetra-</a>) + -logia (see <a href=/index.php?term=-logy&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>-logy</a>). A group of four dramatic compositions, originally three tragedies
(the trilogia) and a Satyric play.
