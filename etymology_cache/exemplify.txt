early 15c., to illustrate by examples, to instruct by (good) example, from Medieval Latin
exemplificare to illustrate, from Latin exemplum (see <a
href=/index.php?term=example&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>example</a>). Meaning to
serve as an example is recorded from 1793. Related: Exemplified; exemplifies; exemplifying.
