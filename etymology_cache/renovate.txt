renovate (v.)

1520s, back-formation from renovation, or else from Latin renovatus, past participle of renovare
renew, restore (see renovation). Related: Renovated; renovating.


gentrify (v.)

renovate inner-city housing to middle-class standards, by 1972, from gentry + -fy. Related:
Gentrified, which was used from early 19c. of persons.
