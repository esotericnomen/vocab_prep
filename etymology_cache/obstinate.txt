mid-14c., from Latin obstinatus resolute, resolved, determined, inflexible, stubborn, past
participle of obstinare persist, stand stubbornly, set one's mind on, from ob by (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ob-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ob-</a>) + stinare (related to
stare stand) from PIE *ste-no-, from root *sta- to stand (see <a
href=/index.php?term=stet&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>stet</a>). Related:
Obstinately.
