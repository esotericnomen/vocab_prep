c.1600, doubtful, questionable, from French problematique (15c.), from Late Latin problematicus,
from Greek problematikos pertaining to a problem, from problematos, genitive of problema (see <a
href=/index.php?term=problem&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>problem</a>). Specific
sense in logic, differentiating what is possible from what is necessarily true, is from 1610s.
Related: Problematical (1560s); problematically.
