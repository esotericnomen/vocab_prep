1792, from French acclimater, verb formed from &agrave; to (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ad-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ad-</a>) + climat (see <a
href=/index.php?term=climate&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>climate</a>). Related:
Acclimated; acclimating. The extended form <a
href=/index.php?term=acclimatize&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>acclimatize</a> is now
more common.
