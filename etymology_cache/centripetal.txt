alternative comb. form of centrum center (see <a href=/index.php?term=center&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>center</a> (n.)) + petere to make for, go to; seek, strive after (see <a
href=/index.php?term=petition&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>petition</a> (n.)).
