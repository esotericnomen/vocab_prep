1520s, from Latin indelebilis indelible, imperishable, from in- not, opposite of, without (see <a
href=/index.php?term=in-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>in-</a> (1)) + delebilis able
to be destroyed, from delere destroy, blot out (see <a
href=/index.php?term=delete&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>delete</a>). Vowel change
from -e- to -i- in English is late 17c. Related: Indelibly.
