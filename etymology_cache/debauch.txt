debauchee (n.)

1660s, from French d&eacute;bauch&eacute; debauched (person), noun use of past participle of
debaucher (see debauch).  <blockquote><b>Debauchee</b>, n. One who has so earnestly pursued pleasure
that he has had the misfortune to overtake it. [Ambrose Bierce, Devil's Dictionary,
1911]</blockquote>
