British name for the tabletop game that in U.S. is <a
href=/index.php?term=checkers&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>checkers</a>, c.1400, from
<a href=/index.php?term=draught&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>draught</a>, perhaps
because the pieces are dragged over the board in moves. Earlier it is recorded as jeu de dames (late
14c.).
