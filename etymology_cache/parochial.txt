late 14c., pertaining to a parish, from Anglo-French parochiel (late 13c.), from Old French
parochial, from Late Latin parochialis of a parish (c.600), from parochia (see <a
href=/index.php?term=parish&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>parish</a>).  <br /><br />
Figurative sense, limited, narrow, as if confined to a small region, is from 1856 (also see <a
href=/index.php?term=parochialism&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>parochialism</a>).
Parochial school is attested from 1755.
