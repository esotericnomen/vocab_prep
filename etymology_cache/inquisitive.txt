inquisitive (adj.)

late 14c., from Old French inquisitif, from Late Latin inquisitivus making inquiry, from Latin
inquisit-, past participle stem of inquirere (see inquire).<blockquote>An housbonde shal nat been
Inquisityf of goddes pryuetee nor of his wyf. [Chaucer, Miller's Prologue]</blockquote>  Related:
Inquisitively; inquisitiveness.
