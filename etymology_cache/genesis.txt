Old English Genesis, first book of the Pentateuch, from Latin genesis, adopted as title of first
book of Old Testament in Vulgate, from Greek genesis origin, creation, generation, from gignesthai
to be born, related to genos race, birth, descent (see <a
href=/index.php?term=genus&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>genus</a>). As such, it
translated Hebrew bereshith, literally in the beginning, which was the first word of the text, taken
as its title. Extended sense of origin, creation first recorded in English c.1600.
