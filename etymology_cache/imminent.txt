present participle of imminere to overhang; impend, be near, be at hand, from assimilated form of
in- into, in, on, upon (see <a href=/index.php?term=in-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>in-</a> (2)) + minere jut out, related to mons hill (see <a
href=/index.php?term=mount&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>mount</a> (n.)). Related:
