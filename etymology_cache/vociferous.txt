1610s, from Latin vociferari to shout, yell, cry out, from vox (genitive vocis) voice  (see <a
href=/index.php?term=voice&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>voice</a> (n.)) + stem of
ferre to carry (see <a href=/index.php?term=infer&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>infer</a>). Related: Vociferously; vociferousness.
