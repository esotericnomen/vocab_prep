mid-15c., from Latin divulgare publish, make common, from dis- apart (see <a
href=/index.php?term=dis-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>dis-</a>) + vulgare make
common property, from vulgus common people (see <a
href=/index.php?term=vulgar&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>vulgar</a>). Related:
Divulged; divulging.
