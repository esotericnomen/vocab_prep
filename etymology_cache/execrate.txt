1560s, from Latin execratus/exsecratus, past participle of execrari/exsecrari to curse, utter a
curse; hate, abhor, from ex- (see <a href=/index.php?term=ex-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>ex-</a>) + sacrare to devote to (see <a
href=/index.php?term=sacred&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>sacred</a>). Hence, to
devote off or away; to curse. Related: Execrated; execrating.
