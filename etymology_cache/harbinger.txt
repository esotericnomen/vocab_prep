German heriberga army shelter) from *heri army (see <a

shelter (see <a href=/index.php?term=barrow&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>barrow</a>
(n.2)). Compare <a href=/index.php?term=harbor&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
