1530s, from Middle French impunit&eacute; (14c.) and directly from Latin impunitatem (nominative
impunitas) freedom from punishment, omission of punishment, also rashness, inconsideration, from
impunis unpunished, without punishment, from assimilated form of in- not, opposite of (see <a
href=/index.php?term=in-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>in-</a> (1)) + poena punishment
(see <a href=/index.php?term=penal&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>penal</a>).
