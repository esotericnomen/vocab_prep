1590s, from Middle French p&eacute;trifier to make or become stone (16c.), from Latin petra rock,
crag (see <a href=/index.php?term=petrous&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>petrous</a>) +
-ficare, from facere to make, do (see <a href=/index.php?term=factitious&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>factitious</a>). Metaphoric sense of paralyze with fear or shock first recorded
1771. Related: <a href=/index.php?term=Petrified&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>Petrified</a>; petrifying.
