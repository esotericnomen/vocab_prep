ontology (n.)

metaphysical science or study of being, 1660s (Gideon Harvey), from Modern Latin ontologia (c.1600),
from onto- + -logy.


ontological (adj.)

1782, from ontology + -ical. Related: Ontologically


paleontology (n.)

1833, probably from French pal&eacute;ontologie, from Greek palaios old, ancient (see paleo-) +
ontologie (see ontology). Related: Paleontological.
