c.1300, abreggen, to make shorter, to condense, from Old French abregier abridge, diminish, shorten,
from Late Latin abbreviare make short (see <a href=/index.php?term=abbreviate&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>abbreviate</a>). The sound development from Latin -vi- to French -dg- is
paralleled in assuage (from assuavidare) and deluge (from diluvium). Related: Abridged; abridging.
