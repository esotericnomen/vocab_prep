late 14c., from Latin digressionem (nominative digressio) a going away, departing, noun of action
from past participle stem of digredi to deviate, from dis- apart, aside (see <a
href=/index.php?term=dis-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>dis-</a>) + gradi to step, go
(see <a href=/index.php?term=grade&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>grade</a> (n.)).
