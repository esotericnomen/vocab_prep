1640s, weak point of a sword blade (contrasted to forte), from French foible (n.), from obsolete
foible (adj.) weak, from Old French foible feeble, dissimilated from Lain flebilis (see <a
href=/index.php?term=feeble&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>feeble</a>). Extended sense
of weak point of character is first recorded 1670s. Related: Foibles.
