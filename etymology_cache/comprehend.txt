catching fire or the arrest of criminals); also to comprehend, perceive (to seize or take in the
mind), from com- completely (see <a href=/index.php?term=com-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>com-</a>) + prehendere to catch hold of, seize (see <a
href=/index.php?term=prehensile&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>prehensile</a>).
