mid-15c., discourse, from Latin colloquium conference, conversation, literally a speaking together,
from com- together (see <a href=/index.php?term=com-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>com-</a>) + -loquium speaking, from loqui to speak (see <a
href=/index.php?term=locution&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>locution</a>). Meaning
conversation is attested in English from 1580s.
