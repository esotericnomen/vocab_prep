venerate (v.)

1620s, back-formation from veneration, or else from Latin veneratus, past participle of venerari to
reverence, worship. Related: Venerated; venerating.


reverence (v.)

late 14c., treat with respect, honor; venerate, pay pious homage to; esteem, value; bow to
(someone); do honor to, from reverence (n.). Related: Reverenced; reverencing.
