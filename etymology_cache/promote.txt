late 14c., one who promotes (the interest of someone), supporter, agent noun from <a
href=/index.php?term=promote&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>promote</a>, and also from
Old French promoteur and directly from Medieval Latin promotor. Specific financial sense of one who
leads in forming a company is from 1876; sense of one who organizes sporting or entertainment events
is attested from 1936.
