1530s, climax, from Middle French gradation (16c.) and directly from Latin gradationem (nominative
gradatio) ascent by steps, a climax, noun of action from gradus step, degree (see <a
href=/index.php?term=grade&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>grade</a> (n.)). Meaning
gradual change is from 1540s. Related: Gradational.
