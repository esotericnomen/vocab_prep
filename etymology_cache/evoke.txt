1620s, from French &eacute;voquer or directly from Latin evocare call out, rouse, summon (see <a
href=/index.php?term=evocation&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>evocation</a>). Often
more or less with a sense of calling spirits, or being called by them. Related: Evoked; evokes;
evoking.
