1640s, an error in computing time or finding dates, from Latin anachronismus, from Greek
anakhronismos, from anakhronizein refer to wrong time, from ana- against (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ana-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ana-</a>) + khronos time (see
<a href=/index.php?term=chrono-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>chrono-</a>). Meaning
something out of harmony with the present first recorded 1816.
