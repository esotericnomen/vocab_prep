1814, from French gastronomie, coined 1800 by Joseph de Berchoux (1762-1838) as title of poem on
good living, after Gastrologia, title of a now-lost poem of antiquity, quoted by Athenaeus (see <a
href=/index.php?term=gastrology&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>gastrology</a>).
Berchoux's word is from gaster + nomos arranging, regulating. Related: Gastronomer.
