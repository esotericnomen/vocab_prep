virologist (n.)

1946; see virology + -ist.


Epstein-Barr virus

1968, named for British virologist Michael Anthony Epstein and Irish-born virologist Yvonne M. Barr.


Salk

in reference to vaccine against poliomyelitis, 1954, from U.S. virologist Jonas Edward Salk
(1914-1995), who developed it.
