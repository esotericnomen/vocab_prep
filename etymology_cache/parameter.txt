1650s in geometry, from Modern Latin parameter (1630s), from Greek para- beside, subsidiary (see <a
href=/index.php?term=para-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>para-</a> (1)) + metron
measure (see <a href=/index.php?term=meter&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>meter</a>
(n.2)).  <br /><br />  A geometry term until 1920s when it yielded sense of measurable factor which
helps to define a particular system (1927). Common modern meaning (influenced by perimeter) of
boundary, limit, characteristic factor is from 1950s. Related: Parametric.
