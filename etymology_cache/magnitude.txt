c.1400, greatness of size or character, from Latin magnitudo greatness, bulk, size, from magnus
great (see <a href=/index.php?term=magnate&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>magnate</a>)
+ -tudo, suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives and participles (see <a
href=/index.php?term=-tude&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>-tude</a>). Meaning size,
extent is from early 15c. Of stars, brightness, from 1640s.
