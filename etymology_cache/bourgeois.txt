bourgeois (adj.)

1560s, of the French middle class, from French bourgeois, from Old French burgeis, borjois town
dweller (see bourgeoisie). Sense of socially or aesthetically conventional is from 1764; in
communist and socialist writing, as a noun, a capitalist (1883).<blockquote>It is better to be a
good ordinary bourgeois than a bad ordinary bohemian. [Aldous Huxley, 1930]</blockquote>
