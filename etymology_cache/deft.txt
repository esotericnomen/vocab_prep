Old English ged&aelig;fte mild, gentle, differentiated in Middle English into <a
href=/index.php?term=daft&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>daft</a> (q.v.) and this word,
via sense of apt, skillful, adept. Cognate with Gothic gadaban to be fit, Old Norse dafna to grow
strong, Dutch deftig important, relevant.
