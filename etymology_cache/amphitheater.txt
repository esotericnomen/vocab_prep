late 14c., from Latin amphitheatrum, from Greek amphitheatron double theater, amphitheater, neuter
of amphitheatros with spectators all around, from amphi- on both sides (see <a
href=/index.php?term=amphi-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>amphi-</a>) + theatron
