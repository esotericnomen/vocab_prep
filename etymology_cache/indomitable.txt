1630s, from Late Latin indomitabilis untameable, from in- not, opposite of, without (see <a
href=/index.php?term=in-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>in-</a> (1)) + *domitabilis,
from Latin domitare, frequentative of domare to tame (see <a
href=/index.php?term=tame&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>tame</a>). Related:
Indomitably.
