1660s, church benefice with an emolument but without parish duties, from Medieval Latin beneficium
sine cura benefice without care (of souls), from Latin sine without (see <a
href=/index.php?term=sans&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>sans</a>) + cura, ablative
singular of cura care (see <a href=/index.php?term=cure&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>cure</a> (n.1)).
