1520s, from Medieval Latin invectiva abusive speech, from Late Latin invectivus abusive, from Latin
invectus, past participle of invehi to attack with words (see <a
href=/index.php?term=inveigh&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>inveigh</a>). For nuances
of usage, see <a href=/index.php?term=humor&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>humor</a>.
The earlier noun form was inveccion (mid-15c.).
