1530s, from Latin recantare recall, revoke, from re- back (see <a
href=/index.php?term=re-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>re-</a>) + cantare to chant
(see <a href=/index.php?term=chant&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>chant</a> (v.)). A
word from the Reformation. Loan-translation of Greek palinoidein recant, from palin back + oeidein
to sing. Related: Recanted; recanting.
