prescient (adj.)

1620s, from Middle French prescient (15c.) and directly from Latin praescientem (nominative
praesciens), present participle of praescire (see prescience).


far-sighted (adj.)

also farsighted, 1640s, forecasting, prescient; 1878 as a defect of the eyes (hypermetropic); see
far + sight. Related: Farsightedness.
