early 14c., line of descent, pedigree, descent, from Old French genealogie (12c.), from Late Latin
genealogia tracing of a family, from Greek genealogia, from genea generation, descent (see <a
href=/index.php?term=genus&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>genus</a>) + -logia (see <a
href=/index.php?term=-logy&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>-logy</a>). An Old English
word for it was folctalu, literally folk tale. Meaning study of family trees is from 1768.
