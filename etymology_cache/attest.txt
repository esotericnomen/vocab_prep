1590s, from Middle French attester (Old French atester, 13c.) affirm, attest, from Latin attestari
confirm, literally bear witness to, from ad- to (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ad-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ad-</a>) + testari bear
witness, from testis witness (see <a href=/index.php?term=testament&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>testament</a>). Related: Attested; attesting.
