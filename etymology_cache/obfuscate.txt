1530s, from Latin obfuscatus, past participle of obfuscare to darken, from ob over (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ob-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ob-</a>) + fuscare to make
dark, from fuscus dark (see <a href=/index.php?term=dusk&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>dusk</a>). Related: Obfuscated; obfuscating.
