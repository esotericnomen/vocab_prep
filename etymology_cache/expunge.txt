c.1600, from Latin expungere prick out, blot out, mark (a name on a list) for deletion by pricking
dots above or below it, literally prick out, from ex- out (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ex-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ex-</a>) + pungere to prick,
stab (see <a href=/index.php?term=pungent&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>pungent</a>).
Related: Expunged; expunging.
