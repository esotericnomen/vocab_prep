1620s, from Medieval Latin heterogeneus, from Greek heterogenes, from heteros different (see <a
href=/index.php?term=hetero-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>hetero-</a>) + genos kind,
gender, race stock (see <a href=/index.php?term=genus&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>genus</a>). Earlier in same sense was heterogeneal (c.1600).
