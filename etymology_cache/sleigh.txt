vehicle mounted on runners for use on ice and snow, 1703, American and Canadian English, from Dutch
slee, shortened from slede (see <a href=/index.php?term=sled&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>sled</a> (n.)). As a verb from 1728. Related: Sleighing. Sleigh-ride is first
recorded 1770; sleigh-bells is from c.1780; they originally were used to give warning of the
approach of a sleigh.
