1610s, from Latin irrefutabilis irrefutable, from assimilated form of in- not, opposite of (see <a
href=/index.php?term=in-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>in-</a> (1)) + refutabilis,
from refutare (see <a href=/index.php?term=refute&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>refute</a>). Related: Irrefutably; irrefutability.
