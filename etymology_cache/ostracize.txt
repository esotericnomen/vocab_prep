1640s, from Greek ostrakizein to banish, literally to banish by voting with potshards (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ostracism&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ostracism</a>).
Figurative sense of to exclude from society is attested from 1640s. Related: Ostracization;
ostracized; ostracizing.
