1570s, beat back, drive back, force back, from Latin reverberatus, past participle of reverberare
strike back, repel, cause to rebound (see <a
href=/index.php?term=reverberation&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>reverberation</a>).
Meaning re-echo is from 1590s. Earlier verb was reverberen (early 15c.). Related: Reverberated;
reverberating.
