mid-15c., from Old French maledicion a curse (15c.), from Latin maledictionem (nominative
maledictio) the action of speaking evil of, slander, in Late Latin a curse, noun of action from past
participle stem of maledicere to speak badly or evil of, slander, from male badly (see <a
href=/index.php?term=mal-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>mal-</a>) + dicere to say (see
<a href=/index.php?term=diction&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>diction</a>).
