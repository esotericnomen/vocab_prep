inopportune (adj.)

1530s, from Late Latin inopportunus unfitting, from in- not + opportunus (see opportune). A rare
word before 19c. Related: Inopportunely.


unseasonable (adj.)

mid-15c., inopportune, from un- (1) not + seasonable (adj.). In reference to weather, not
appropriate to the time of year, it is recorded from 1510s. Related: Unseasonably.
