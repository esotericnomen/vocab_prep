c.1400, from Latin circumlocutionem (nominative circumlocutio) a speaking around (the topic), from
circum- around (see <a href=/index.php?term=circum-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>circum-</a>) + locutionem (nominative locutio) a speaking, noun of action from
past participle stem of loqui to speak (see <a href=/index.php?term=locution&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>locution</a>). A loan-translation of Greek periphrasis (see <a
href=/index.php?term=periphrasis&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>periphrasis</a>).
