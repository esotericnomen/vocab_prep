c.1600, belonging to the earliest age, from Medieval Latin primalis primary, from Latin primus first
(see <a href=/index.php?term=prime&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>prime</a> (adj.)).
Psychological sense, in reference to Freud's theory of behaviors springing from the earliest stage
of emotional development, is attested from 1918. Primal scream is from a best-selling book of 1971.
