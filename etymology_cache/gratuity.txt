1520s, graciousness, from French gratuit&eacute; (14c.) or directly from Medieval Latin gratuitatem
(nominative gratuitas) free gift, probably from Latin gratuitus free, freely given (see <a
href=/index.php?term=gratuitous&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>gratuitous</a>). Meaning
money given for favor or services is first attested 1530s.
