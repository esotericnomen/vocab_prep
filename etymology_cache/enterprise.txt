early 15c., an undertaking, from Old French enterprise an undertaking, noun use of fem. past
participle of entreprendre undertake, take in hand, from entre- between (see <a
href=/index.php?term=entre-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>entre-</a>) + prendre to
take, contraction of prehendere (see <a href=/index.php?term=prehensile&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
