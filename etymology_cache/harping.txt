Old English hearpian; see <a href=/index.php?term=harp&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>harp</a> (n.). Cognate with Middle Dutch, Dutch harpen, Middle High German
harpfen, German harfen. Figurative sense of talk overmuch (about something) first recorded mid-15c.,
originally to harp upon one string. Related: Harped; harping.
