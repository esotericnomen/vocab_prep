1640s, from Latin elicitus, past participle of elicere draw forth, from ex- out (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ex-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ex-</a>) + -licere, comb. form

href=/index.php?term=lace&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>lace</a> (n.)). Related:
