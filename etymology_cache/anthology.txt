1630s, collection of poetry, from Latin anthologia, from Greek anthologia collection of small poems
and epigrams by several authors, literally flower-gathering, from anthos a flower (see <a
href=/index.php?term=anther&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>anther</a>) + logia
collection, collecting, from legein gather (see <a
href=/index.php?term=lecture&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>lecture</a> (n.)). Modern
sense (which emerged in Late Greek) is metaphoric, flowers of verse, small poems by various writers
gathered together.
