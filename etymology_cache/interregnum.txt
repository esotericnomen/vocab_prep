1570s, from Latin interregnum, literally between-reign, from inter- (see <a
href=/index.php?term=inter-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>inter-</a>) + regnum (see <a
href=/index.php?term=reign&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>reign</a> (n.)). In the
republic, a vacancy in the consulate.
