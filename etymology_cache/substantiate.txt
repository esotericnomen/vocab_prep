1650s, to make real, to give substance to, from Modern Latin substantiatus, past participle of
substantiare, from Latin substantia being, essence, material (see <a
href=/index.php?term=substance&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>substance</a>). Meaning
to demonstrate or prove is attested from 1803. Related: Substantiated; substantiating.
