early 14c., harsh or severe treatment, from Old French duresse, from Latin duritia hardness, from
durus hard (see <a href=/index.php?term=endure&amp;allowed_in_frame=0

adjectives to form nouns of quality (compare riches, largesse). Sense of coercion, compulsion is
from 1590s.
