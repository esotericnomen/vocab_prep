1560s, back-formation from exclamation or else from Middle French exclamer (16c.), from Latin
exclamare cry out loud, from ex- intensive prefix out (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ex-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ex-</a>) + clamare cry, shout,
call (see <a href=/index.php?term=claim&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>claim</a> (v.)).
Spelling influenced by claim. Related: Exclaimed; exclaiming.
