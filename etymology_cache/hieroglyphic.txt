1580s (adj. and n.), from Late Latin hieroglyphicus, from Greek hieroglyphikos hieroglyphic; of
Egyptian writing, from hieros sacred (see <a href=/index.php?term=ire&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>ire</a>) + glyphe carving, from glyphein to carve (see <a
href=/index.php?term=glyph&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>glyph</a>). Plutarch began
the custom of using the adjective (ta hieroglyphika) as a noun.
