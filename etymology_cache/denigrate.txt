1520s, from Latin denigratus, past participle of denigrare to blacken, defame, from de- completely
(see <a href=/index.php?term=de-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>de-</a>) + nigr-, stem
of niger black (see <a href=/index.php?term=Negro&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>Negro</a>). of unknown origin. Apparently disused in 18th c. and revived in
19th c. [OED]. Related: Denigrated; denigrating.
