1570s, from Latin exudare/exsudare ooze out like sweat, from ex- out (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ex-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ex-</a>) + sudare to sweat,
from sudor sweat (see <a href=/index.php?term=sweat&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>sweat</a> (v.)). Related: Exuded; exudes; exuding.
