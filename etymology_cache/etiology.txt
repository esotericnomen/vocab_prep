etiology (n.)

science of causes or causation, 1550s, from Late Latin aetiologia, from Greek aitiologia statement
of cause, from aitia cause + -logia a speaking (see -logy). Related: Etiologic; etiological.


aetio-

word-forming element used in chemistry and indicating a fundamental degradation product of a complex
organic compound [Flood], from Latinized comb. form of Greek aitia a cause, an origin (see
etiology).
