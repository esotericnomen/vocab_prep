1550s, from Middle French fusion, from Latin fusionem (nominative fusio) an outpouring, effusion,
noun of action from fusus, past participle of fundere pour, melt (see <a
href=/index.php?term=found&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>found</a> (v.2)). In nuclear
physics sense, first recorded 1947; in jazz sense, by 1972.
