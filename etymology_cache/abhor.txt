1610s, in a position or condition to recoil, usually with from; from Latin abhorentem (nominative
abhorrens), present participle of abhorrere; see <a
href=/index.php?term=abhor&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>abhor</a>. Meaning repugnant
