1620s, insomnie, from Latin insomnia want of sleep, from insomnis sleepless, from in- not (see <a
href=/index.php?term=in-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>in-</a> (1)) + somnus sleep
(see <a href=/index.php?term=Somnus&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>Somnus</a>). The
modern form is from 1758.
