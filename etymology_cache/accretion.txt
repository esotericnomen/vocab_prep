1610s, from Latin accretionem (nominative accretio) an increasing, a growing larger (as of the
waxing moon), noun of action from past participle stem of accrescere, from ad- to (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ad-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ad-</a>) + crescere grow (see
<a href=/index.php?term=crescent&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>crescent</a>).
