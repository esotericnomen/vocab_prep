1590s to banish, send into exile, from Latin relegatus, past participle of relegare remove, dismiss,
banish, send away, schedule, put aside, from re- back (see <a
href=/index.php?term=re-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>re-</a>) + legare send with a
commission (see <a href=/index.php?term=legate&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>legate</a>). Meaning place in a position of inferiority is recorded from 1790.
Related: Relegated; relegating; relegable.
