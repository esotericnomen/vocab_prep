to make thin, to make less, 1520s, from Latin attenuatus enfeebled, weak, past participle of
attenuare to make thin, lessen, diminish, from ad- to (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ad-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ad-</a>) + tenuare make thin,
from tenuis thin (see <a href=/index.php?term=tenet&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>tenet</a>). Related: Attenuated; attenuating. Earlier was Middle English
attenuen to make thin (in consistency), early 15c.
