1660s, painting or drawing done in different tints of a single color, from Greek monochromos of a
single color, from monos single, alone (see <a href=/index.php?term=mono-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>mono-</a>) + khroma (genitive khromatos) color, complexion, skin (see <a
href=/index.php?term=chroma&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>chroma</a>). As an adjective
from 1849. Photographic sense is recorded from 1940.
