early 15c., action of manifesting; exhibition, demonstration, from Late Latin manifestationem
(nominative manifestatio), noun of action from past participle stem of Latin manifestare (see <a
href=/index.php?term=manifest&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>manifest</a> (adj.)).
Meaning an object, action, or presence by which something is made manifest is from 1785. The
spiritualism sense is attested from 1853.
