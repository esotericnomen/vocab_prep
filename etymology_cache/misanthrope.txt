one who hates mankind, 1560s, from Greek misanthropos hating mankind, from misein to hate (see <a
href=/index.php?term=miso-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>miso-</a>) + anthropos man
(see <a href=/index.php?term=anthropo-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>anthropo-</a>).
Alternative form misanthropist is attested from 1650s.
