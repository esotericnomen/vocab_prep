mid-13c., clear (rubbish, grass, etc.) by raking; gather (grain) by raking, from <a
href=/index.php?term=rake&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>rake</a> (n.1), or from a lost
Old English verb related to it, or from a similar Scandinavian source (compare Swedish raka, Danish

from 1580s.
