1650s, of the third order, rank, degree, etc., from Latin tertiarius of or pertaining to a third,
from tertius third, a third, from root of tres three (see <a
href=/index.php?term=three&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>three</a>). The geological
sense (with capital T-) of era after the Mesozoic (which formerly was called the Secondary) is
attested from 1794, after Italian terziari, used in this sense 1760 by Italian geologist Giovanni
Arduino (1714-1795).
