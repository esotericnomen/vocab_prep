Old English floterian to flutter, fly, flicker, float to and fro, be tossed by waves, frequentative
of flotian to float (see <a href=/index.php?term=float&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>float</a> (v.)). Related: Fluttered; fluttering. As a noun from 1640s; meaning
state of excitement is 1740s.
