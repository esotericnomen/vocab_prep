1847, pertaining to the relation between mind and body, from Greek psykhe- mind (see <a
href=/index.php?term=psyche&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>psyche</a>) + somatikos,
from soma (genitive somatos) body (see <a href=/index.php?term=somato-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>somato-</a>). Applied from 1938 to physical disorders with psychological
causes. Etymologically it could as easily apply to emotional disorders with physical causes, but it
is rarely used as such.
