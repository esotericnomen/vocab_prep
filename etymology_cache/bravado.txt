1580s, from French bravade bragging, boasting, from Italian bravata bragging, boasting (16c.), from
bravare brag, boast, be defiant, from bravo (see <a
href=/index.php?term=brave&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>brave</a> (adj.)). The
English word was influenced in form by Spanish words ending in <a
