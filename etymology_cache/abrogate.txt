1520s, from Latin abrogatus, past participle of abrogare to annul, repeal (a law), from ab- away
(see <a href=/index.php?term=ab-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ab-</a>) + rogare
propose a law, request (see <a href=/index.php?term=rogation&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>rogation</a>). Form abrogen, from Old French abroger, is recorded from early
15c. Related: Abrogated; abrogating.
