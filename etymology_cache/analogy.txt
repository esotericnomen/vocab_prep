1540s (perhaps early 15c.), from Old French analogie or directly from Latin analogia, from Greek
analogia proportion, from ana- upon, according to (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ana-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ana-</a>) + logos ratio, also
word, speech, reckoning (see <a href=/index.php?term=logos&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>logos</a>). A mathematical term used in a wider sense by Plato.
