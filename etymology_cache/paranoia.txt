paranoia (n.)

mental disorder characterized by systematized delusions, 1848 (earlier paranoea 1811), from Greek
paranoia mental derangement, madness, from paranoos mentally ill, insane, from para- beside, beyond
(see para- (1)) + noos mind.  <blockquote>  FOR several years frequent descriptions have been given
in the foreign journals, especially German and Italian, of the forms of insanity designated by the
names Paranoia, Verr&#252;cktkeit, and Wahnsinn. [Paranoia -- Systematized Delusions and Mental
Degenerations, J. S&#233;glas (transl. William Noyes), 1888]  </blockquote>
