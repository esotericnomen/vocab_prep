1828, manager or promoter of a theatrical production, reborrowing of French entrepreneur one who
undertakes or manages, agent noun from Old French entreprendre undertake (see <a
href=/index.php?term=enterprise&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>enterprise</a>). The
word first crossed the Channel late 15c. but did not stay. Meaning business manager is from 1852.
Related: Entrepreneurship.
