1911, study of the occult meaning of numbers, a hybrid from Latin numerus a number (see <a
href=/index.php?term=number&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>number</a> (n.)) + Greek
-logia (see <a href=/index.php?term=-logy&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>-logy</a>). A
correct formation would be arithmology, from Greek arithmos number. Related: Numerological;
numerologist.
