1530s, porage soup of meat and vegetables, alteration of <a
href=/index.php?term=pottage&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>pottage</a>, perhaps from
influence of Middle English porray, porreie leek broth, from Old French poree leek soup, from Vulgar
Latin *porrata, from Latin porrum leek. Spelling with -idge attested from c.1600. Association with
oatmeal is 1640s, first in Scottish.
