1620s, flat thin tablet used by an artist to lay and mix colors, from French palette, from Old
French palete small shovel, blade (13c.) diminutive of pale shovel, blade, from Latin pala spade,
shoulder blade, probably from PIE *pak-slo-, from root *pag- (see <a
href=/index.php?term=pact&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>pact</a>). Transferred sense
of colors used by a particular artist is from 1882.
