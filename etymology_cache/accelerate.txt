1520s, from Latin acceleratus, past participle of accelerare to hasten, to quicken, from ad- to (see
<a href=/index.php?term=ad-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ad-</a>) + celerare hasten,
from celer swift (see <a href=/index.php?term=celerity&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>celerity</a>). Related: Accelerated; accelerating.
