before Noah's flood, 1640s, formed from Latin ante- before (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ante&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ante</a>) + diluvium a flood
(see <a href=/index.php?term=deluge&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>deluge</a> (n.)).
Coined by English physician Sir Thomas Browne (1605-1682). As a noun meaning person who lived before
the Flood, from 1680s.
