1733, from Latin annotatus, past participle of annotare to note down (see <a
href=/index.php?term=annotation&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>annotation</a>).
Related: Annotated; annotating. Not in Johnson's Dictionary, but used therein in defining comment.
Form annote is recorded from mid-15c. Related: Annotated; annotating.
