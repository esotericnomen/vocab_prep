mid-15c., eloquent, from Latin rhetoricus, from Greek rhetorikos oratorical, rhetorical; skilled in
speaking, from rhetor orator (see <a href=/index.php?term=rhetoric&amp;allowed_in_frame=0

question is from 1670s. Related: Rhetorically.
