c.1600, from Latin abstemius sober, temperate, from ab(s)- from (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ab-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ab-</a>) + stem of temetum
strong drink, related to temulentus drunken. Technically, of liquor, but extended in Latin to
temperance in living generally. Related: Abstemiously; abstemiousness.
