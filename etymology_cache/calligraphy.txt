1610s, from Greek kaligraphia, from kallos beauty (see <a
href=/index.php?term=Callisto&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>Callisto</a>) + graphein
to write (see <a href=/index.php?term=-graphy&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>-graphy</a>). Related: Calligraphic. The usual comb. form in Greek was kalli-
beautiful, fine, happy, favorable; kalo- was a later, rarer alternative form.
