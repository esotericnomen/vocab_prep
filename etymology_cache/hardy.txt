or make bold, from Frankish *hardjan to make hard (cognates: Old Frisian herda, Old High German
herten, Old Norse her&eth;a, Gothic gahardjan make hard), from Proto-Germanic *hardu- (see <a

English <a href=/index.php?term=hard&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>hard</a>. Related:
Hardily; hardiness. Hardhede physical hardiness is attested from early 15c.
