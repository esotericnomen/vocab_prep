1797, from German Ph&auml;nomenologie, used as the title of the fourth part of the Neues Organon of
German physicist Johann Heinrich Lambert (1728-1777), coined from Greek phainomenon (see <a
href=/index.php?term=phenomenon&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>phenomenon</a>) + -logia
(see <a href=/index.php?term=-logy&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>-logy</a>).
Psychological sense, especially in Gestalt theory, is from 1930. Related: Phenomenological.
