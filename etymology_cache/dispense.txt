c.1400, one who administers (a household, etc.), c.1200 in surnames, from Anglo-French dispensour,
Old French despenseor, from Latin dispensatorem, agent noun from dispensare (see <a
href=/index.php?term=dispense&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>dispense</a>). Meaning a
container that dispenses in fixed measure is from 1918.
