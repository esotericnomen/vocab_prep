1640s, payment for saving a ship from wreck or capture, from French salvage (15c.), from Old French
salver to save (see <a href=/index.php?term=save&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>save</a> (v.)). The general sense of the saving of property from danger is
attested from 1878. Meaning recycling of waste material is from 1918, from the British effort in
World War I.
