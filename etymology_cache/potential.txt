late 14c., possible (as opposed to actual), from Old French potenciel and directly from Late Latin
potentialis potential, from Latin potentia power, might, force; figuratively political power,
authority, influence, from potens powerful (see <a
href=/index.php?term=potent&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>potent</a>). The noun,
meaning that which is possible, is first attested 1817, from the adjective.
