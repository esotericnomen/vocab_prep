late 14c., exorcism, from Late Latin adjurationem (nominative adjuratio) a swearing to, noun of
action from past participle stem of adjurare (see <a
href=/index.php?term=adjure&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>adjure</a>). Originally a
