mid-15c., evil speaking, from Late Latin obloquium speaking against, contradiction, from Latin
obloqui to speak against, contradict, from ob against (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ob-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ob-</a>) + loqui to speak, from
PIE *tolk(w)- to speak (see <a href=/index.php?term=locution&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
