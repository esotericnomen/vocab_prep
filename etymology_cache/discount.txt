1620s, abatement, alteration of 16c. French descompte, from Medieval Latin discomputus (source of
Italian disconto), from discomputare (see <a href=/index.php?term=discount&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>discount</a> (v.)). Meaning deduction for early payment is from 1680s; meaning
reduction in the price of goods attested by 1837.
