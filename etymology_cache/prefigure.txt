early 15c., from Late Latin praefigurare to prefigure, from Latin prae before (see <a
href=/index.php?term=pre-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>pre-</a>) + figurare to form,
shape, from figura a shape, form, figure (see <a href=/index.php?term=figure&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
