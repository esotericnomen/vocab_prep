1580s, from Middle French dissection, from Medieval Latin dissectionem (nominative dissectio), noun
of action from past participle stem of Latin dissecare cut in pieces, from dis- apart (see <a
href=/index.php?term=dis-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>dis-</a>) + secare to cut (see
<a href=/index.php?term=section&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>section</a>).
