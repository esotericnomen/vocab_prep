1660s, from Latin dictum thing said (a saying, bon-mot, prophecy, etc.), an order, command, neuter
of dictus, past participle of dicere say (see <a href=/index.php?term=diction&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>diction</a>). In legal use, a judge's expression of opinion which is not the
formal resolution of a case.
