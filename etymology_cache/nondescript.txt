1680s, not hitherto described, in scientific usage, coined from <a
href=/index.php?term=non-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>non-</a> + Latin descriptus,
past participle of describere (see <a href=/index.php?term=describe&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>describe</a>). General sense of not easily described or classified is from
1806.
