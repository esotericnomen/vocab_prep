1620s, to avenge or revenge, from Latin vindicatus, past participle of vindicare to stake a claim;
to liberate; to act as avenger (see <a href=/index.php?term=vindication&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>vindication</a>). Meaning to clear from censure or doubt, by means of
demonstration is recorded from 1630s. Related: Vindicated, vindicating.
