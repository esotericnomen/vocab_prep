late 14c., a prohibition; absence, nonexistence; opposite, from Old French negatif and directly from
Latin negativus (see <a href=/index.php?term=negative&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>negative</a> (adj.)). Meaning a negative statement is from 1560s. As a
response, I refuse, disagree, no, from 1945. Meaning a negative quality is from 1640s. In
mathematics, a negative number, from 1706. Photographic sense first recorded 1853.
