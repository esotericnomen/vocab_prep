1590s, from Middle French abstrus (16c.) or directly from Latin abstrusus hidden, concealed, secret,
past participle of abstrudere conceal, literally to thrust away, from ab- away (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ab-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ab-</a>) + trudere to thrust,
push (see <a href=/index.php?term=extrusion&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>extrusion</a>). Related: Abstrusely; abstruseness.
