early 15c., to roll down, from Latin devolvere to roll down, from de- (see <a
href=/index.php?term=de-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>de-</a>) + volvere to roll (see
<a href=/index.php?term=volvox&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>volvox</a>). Figurative
sense of to cause to pass down is from 1520s. Related: Devolved; devolving. Also in same sense was
devolute (1530s), from Latin devolutus, past participle of devolvere.
