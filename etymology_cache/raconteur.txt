storyteller, person skilled in relating anecdotes, 1828, from French raconteur, from raconter to
recount, tell, narrate, from re- (see <a href=/index.php?term=re-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>re-</a>) + Old French aconter to count, render account (see <a
href=/index.php?term=account&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>account</a> (v.); and
compare <a href=/index.php?term=recount&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>recount</a>
(v.1)). Related: Raconteuse (fem.).
