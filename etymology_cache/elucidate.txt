elucidate (v.)

1560s, perhaps via Middle French &eacute;lucider (15c.) or directly from Late Latin elucidatus, past
participle of elucidare make clear, from ex- out, away (see ex-) + lucidus clear (see lucid).
Related: Elucidated; elucidates; elucidating.


elucidation (n.)

1560s, noun of action from elucidate.


declare (v.)

early 14c., from Old French declarer explain, elucidate, or directly from Latin declarare make
clear, reveal, disclose, announce, from de- intensive prefix (see de-) + clarare clarify, from
clarus clear (see clear (adj.)). Related: Declared; declaring.
