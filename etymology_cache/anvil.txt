Old English anfilt, a Proto-Germanic compound (cognates: Middle Dutch anvilt, Old High German
anafalz, Dutch aanbeeld, Danish ambolt anvil) from *ana- on + *filtan hit (see <a
href=/index.php?term=felt&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>felt</a> (n.)). The ear bone
so called from 1680s. Anvil Chorus is based on the Gypsy Song that opens Act II of Giuseppe Verdi's
opera Il Trovatore, first performed in Teatro Apollo, Rome, Jan. 19, 1853.
