1540s, from Latin astringentum (nominative astringens), present participle of astringere to bind
fast, tighten, contract, from ad- to (see <a href=/index.php?term=ad-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>ad-</a>) + stringere draw tight (see <a
href=/index.php?term=strain&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>strain</a> (v.)). As a noun
from 1620s.
