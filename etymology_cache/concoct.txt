1530s, digestion, from Latin concoctionem (nominative concoctio) digestion, noun of action from past
participle stem of concoquere (see <a href=/index.php?term=concoct&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>concoct</a>). Meaning preparation of a medicinal potion is from 1851; sense of
a made-up story is from 1823.
