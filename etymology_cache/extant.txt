1540s, standing out above a surface, from Latin extantem (nominative extans), present participle of
extare stand out, be visible, exist, from ex- out (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ex-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ex-</a>) + stare to stand, from
PIE root *sta- to stand (see <a href=/index.php?term=stet&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>stet</a>). Sense of in existence attested in English by 1560s.
