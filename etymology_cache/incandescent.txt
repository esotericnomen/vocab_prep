1794, from French incandescent or directly from Latin incandescentem (nominative incandescens),
present participle of incandescere become warm, glow, kindle, from in- within (see <a
href=/index.php?term=in-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>in-</a> (2)) + candescere begin
to glow, become white, inceptive of candere to glow, to shine (see <a
