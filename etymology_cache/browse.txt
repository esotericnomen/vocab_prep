browser (n.)

1845, animal which browses, agent noun from browse (v.). In the computer sense by 1982.
<blockquote>  The first browser was invented at PARC by Larry Tesler, now a designer at Apple
Computer. Tesler's first Smalltalk browser was a tree-structured device. It enabled programmers to
hunt quickly for items in a Smalltalk dictionary. [InfoWorld magazine, vol. v., no. 4, Jan. 24,
1983]  </blockquote>
