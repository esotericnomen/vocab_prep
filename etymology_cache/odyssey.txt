c.1600, Odyssey, from Latin Odyssea, from Greek Odysseia, name of the Homeric epic poem of ancient
Greece, relating the ten-year wanderings of <a href=/index.php?term=Odysseus&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>Odysseus</a>, king of Ithaca, after the Trojan War. Figurative sense of long,
adventurous journey is first recorded 1889.
