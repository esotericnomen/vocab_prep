ego (n.)

1714, as a term in metaphysics, from Latin ego I (cognate with Old English ic, see I).
Psychoanalytic sense is from 1894; sense of conceit is 1891. Ego trip first recorded 1969.
<blockquote>  In the book of Egoism it is written, Possession without obligation to the object
possessed approaches felicity. [George Meredith, The Egoist, 1879]  </blockquote>
