1714, as a term in metaphysics, from Latin ego I (cognate with Old English ic, see <a
href=/index.php?term=I&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>I</a>). Psychoanalytic sense is
from 1894; sense of conceit is 1891. Ego trip first recorded 1969.  <blockquote>  In the book of
Egoism it is written, Possession without obligation to the object possessed approaches felicity.
[George Meredith, The Egoist, 1879]  </blockquote>
