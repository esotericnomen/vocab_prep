to another, from ad- to (see <a href=/index.php?term=ad-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>ad-</a>) + commodare make fit, from commodus fit (see <a
href=/index.php?term=commode&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>commode</a>). Related:
Accommodated; <a href=/index.php?term=accommodating&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
