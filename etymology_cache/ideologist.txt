1815, in reference to the French Revolutionaries, from French ideologue, from Greek idea (see <a
href=/index.php?term=idea&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>idea</a>) + -logos (see <a
href=/index.php?term=-logue&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>-logue</a>). Earlier form
was ideologist (1798).
