1580s, relating to an academy, also collegiate, scholarly, from Latin academicus of the Academy,
from academia (see <a href=/index.php?term=academy&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>academy</a>). Meaning theoretical, not practical, not leading to a decision
(such as university debates or classroom legal exercises) is from 1886. Academic freedom is attested
from 1901. Related: Academically.
