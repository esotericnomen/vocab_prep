early 15c., from Medieval Latin exhumare to unearth (13c.), from Latin ex- out of (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ex-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ex-</a>) + humare bury, from
humus earth (see <a href=/index.php?term=chthonic&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>chthonic</a>). An alternative form was exhumate (1540s), taken directly from
Medieval Latin. Related: Exhumed; exhuming.
