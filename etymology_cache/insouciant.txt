1829, from French insouciant careless, thoughtless, heedless, from in- not (see <a
href=/index.php?term=in-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>in-</a> (1)) + souciant caring,
present participle of soucier to care, from Latin sollicitare to agitate (see <a
href=/index.php?term=solicit&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>solicit</a>). Related:
Insouciantly.
