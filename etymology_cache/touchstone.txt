late 15c., from <a href=/index.php?term=touch&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>touch</a>
(v.) in the Middle English sense to test (metal) + <a
href=/index.php?term=stone&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>stone</a> (n.). Fine-grained
black quartz, used for testing the quality of gold and silver alloys by the color of the streak made
by rubbing them on it. Also see <a href=/index.php?term=basalt&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>basalt</a>. Figurative sense is from 1530s.
