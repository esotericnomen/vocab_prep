1650s, back-formation from amalgamation, or from adjective amalgamate (1640s) from <a
href=/index.php?term=amalgam&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>amalgam</a>. Originally in
metallurgy; figurative sense of to unite (races, etc.) is attested from 1802. Related: Amalgamated;
amalgamating. Earlier verb was amalgamen (1540s).
