1610s, from Latin automaton (Suetonius), from Greek automaton, neuter of automatos self-acting, from
autos self (see <a href=/index.php?term=auto-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>auto-</a>)
+ matos thinking, animated, willing, from PIE *mn-to-, from root *men- to think (see <a
href=/index.php?term=mind&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>mind</a> (n.)).
