mid-15c., from Latin inviolabilis inviolable, invulnerable, from in- not, opposite of (see <a
href=/index.php?term=in-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>in-</a> (1)) + violabilis, from
violare to do violence to (see <a href=/index.php?term=violation&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
