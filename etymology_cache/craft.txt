Old English cr&aelig;ftan to exercise a craft, build, from the same source as <a
href=/index.php?term=craft&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>craft</a> (n.). Meaning to
make skilfully is from early 15c., obsolete from 16c., but revived c.1950s, largely in U.S.
advertising and commercial senses. Related: Crafted; crafting.
