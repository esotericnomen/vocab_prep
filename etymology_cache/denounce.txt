early 14c., announce, from Old French denoncier (12c., Modern French d&eacute;noncer), from Latin
denuntiare to announce, proclaim; denounce, menace; command, order, from de- down + nuntiare
proclaim, announce, from nuntius messenger (see <a
href=/index.php?term=nuncio&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>nuncio</a>). Negative sense
in English via meaning to declare or proclaim something as cursed, excommunicated, forgiven, removed
from office. Related: Denounced; denouncing.
