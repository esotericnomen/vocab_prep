psychiatrist (n.)

1875, from psychiatry + -ist.<blockquote>A psychiatrist is a man who goes to the Folies
Berg&egrave;re and looks at the audience. [Anglican Bishop Mervyn Stockwood, 1961]</blockquote>  An
older name was mad-doctor (1703); also psychiater expert in mental diseases (1852), from Greek
psyche + iatros. Also see alienist.
