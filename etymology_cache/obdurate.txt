mid-15c., stubborn; hardened, from Latin obduratus hardened, past participle of obdurare be hard,
hold out, persist, endure, from ob against (see <a href=/index.php?term=ob-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>ob-</a>) + durare harden, render hard, from durus hard (see <a
href=/index.php?term=endure&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>endure</a>). Related:
Obdurately.
