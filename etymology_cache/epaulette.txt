epaulet (n.)

also epaulette, shoulder ornament on a uniform, 1783, from French &eacute;paulette an epaulet
(16c.), diminutive of &eacute;paule shoulder, from Old French espaule (12c.), from Latin spatula
flat piece of wood, splint, in Medieval Latin shoulder blade, diminutive of spatha broad wooden
instrument, broad sword, from Greek spathe a broad flat sword (see spade (n.1)).
