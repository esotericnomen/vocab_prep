1650s, a begining with the same letter, from Modern Latin alliterationem (nominative alliteratio),
noun of action from past participle stem of alliterare to begin with the same letter, from Latin ad-
to (see <a href=/index.php?term=ad-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ad-</a>) + littera
(also litera) letter, script (see <a href=/index.php?term=letter&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>letter</a>). Formed on model of obliteration, etc. Related: Alliterational.
