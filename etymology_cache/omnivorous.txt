1650s, from Latin omnivorus all-devouring, from omnis all (see <a
href=/index.php?term=omni&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>omni</a>-) + vorare devour,
swallow (see <a href=/index.php?term=voracity&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>voracity</a>). Related: Omnivorously; omnivorousness.
