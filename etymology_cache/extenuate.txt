1520s, from Latin extenuatus, past participle of extenuare lessen, make small, reduce, diminish,
from ex- out (see <a href=/index.php?term=ex-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ex-</a>) +
tenuare make thin, from tenuis thin (see <a href=/index.php?term=tenet&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>tenet</a>). Related: Extenuated; extenuating. Extenuating circumstances
attested from 1660s.
