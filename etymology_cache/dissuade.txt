argument, from dis- off, against (see <a href=/index.php?term=dis-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>dis-</a>) + suadere to urge (see <a
href=/index.php?term=suasion&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>suasion</a>). Related:
Dissuaded; dissuading.
