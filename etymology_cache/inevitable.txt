mid-15c., from Latin inevitabilis unavoidable, from in- not, opposite of (see <a
href=/index.php?term=in-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>in-</a> (1)) + evitabilis
avoidable, from evitare to avoid, from ex- out (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ex-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ex-</a>) + vitare shun,
originally go out of the way.
