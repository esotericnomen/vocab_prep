execrable (adj.)

late 14c., from Old French execrable, from Latin execrabilis/exsecrabilis execrable, accursed, from
execrari/exsecrari (see execrate). Related: Execrably.


detestable (adj.)

early 15c., from Middle French d&eacute;testable (14c.), from Latin detestabilis execrable,
abominable, from detestari (see detest). Related: Detestably.
