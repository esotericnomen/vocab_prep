1550s, coined erroneously in Greek as phraseologia (1550s), from Greek phrasis way of speaking (see
<a href=/index.php?term=phrase&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>phrase</a> (n.)) + -logia
(see <a href=/index.php?term=-logy&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>-logy</a>). The
correct form would be *phrasiology. Originally a phrase book, meaning way of arranging words,
characteristic style of expression is from 1660s.
