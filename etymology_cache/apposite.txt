fit, proper, suitable, past participle of apponere apply to, put near, from ad- near (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ad-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ad-</a>) + ponere to place (see
<a href=/index.php?term=position&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>position</a> (n.)).
