1873, branch of science dealing with the relationship of living things to their environments, coined
by German zoologist Ernst Haeckel (1834-1919) as Okologie, from Greek oikos house, dwelling place,
habitation (see <a href=/index.php?term=villa&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>villa</a>)
+ -logia study of (see <a href=/index.php?term=-logy&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>-logy</a>). In use with reference to anti-pollution activities from 1960s.
