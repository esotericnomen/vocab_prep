1822, from Greek demotikos of or for the common people, in common use, from demos common people,
originally district, from PIE *da-mo- division, from root *da- to divide (see <a
href=/index.php?term=tide&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>tide</a>). In contrast to <a
href=/index.php?term=hieratic&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>hieratic</a>. Originally
of the simpler of two forms of ancient Egyptian writing; broader sense is from 1831; used of Greek
since 1927.
