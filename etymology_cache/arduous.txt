1530s, hard to accomplish, difficult to do, from Latin arduus high, steep, also figuratively,
difficult, from PIE root *eredh- to grow, high (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ortho-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ortho-</a>). Literal sense
of high, steep, difficult to climb, attested in English from 1709.
