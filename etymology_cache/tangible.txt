1580s, capable of being touched, from Middle French tangible and directly from Late Latin tangibilis
that may be touched, from Latin tangere to touch (see <a
href=/index.php?term=tangent&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>tangent</a> (adj.)). Sense
of material (as in tangible reward) is first recorded 1610s; that of able to be realized or dealt
with is from 1709. Related: Tangibly.
