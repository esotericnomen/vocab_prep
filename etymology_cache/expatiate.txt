1530s, walk about, roam freely, from Latin expatiatus/exspatiatus, past participle of
expatiari/exspatiari wander, digress, from ex- out (see <a
href=/index.php?term=ex-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ex-</a>) + spatiari to walk,
spread out, from spatium (see <a href=/index.php?term=space&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>space</a>). Meaning talk or write at length is 1610s. Related: Expatiated;
expatiating.
