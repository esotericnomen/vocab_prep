1610s, from French d&eacute;clivit&eacute;, from Latin declivitatem (nominative declivitas) a slope,
declivity, from declivis a sloping downward, from de- down (see <a
href=/index.php?term=de-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>de-</a>) + clivus a slope, from
PIE *klei-wo-, suffixed form of *klei to lean (see <a
href=/index.php?term=lean&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>lean</a> (v.)).
