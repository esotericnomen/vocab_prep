1847, mental derangement, Modern Latin, from Greek psykhe- mind (see <a
href=/index.php?term=psyche&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>psyche</a>) + <a
href=/index.php?term=-osis&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>-osis</a> abnormal condition.
Greek psykhosis meant a giving of life; animation; principle of life.
