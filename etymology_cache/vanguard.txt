mid-15c., vaunt garde, from Middle French avant-garde, from avant in front (see <a
href=/index.php?term=avant&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>avant</a>) + garde guard (see
<a href=/index.php?term=guard&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>guard</a> (n.)). Communist
revolutionary sense is recorded from 1928.
