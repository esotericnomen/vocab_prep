mid-15c., from Latin exoneratus, past participle of exonerare remove a burden, discharge, unload,
from ex- off (see <a href=/index.php?term=ex-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>ex-</a>) +
onerare to unload; overload, oppress, from onus (genitive oneris) burden (see <a
href=/index.php?term=onus&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>onus</a>). Related:
Exonerated; exonerating.
