early 14c., from Old French restitucion or directly from Latin restitutionem (nominative restitutio)
a restoring, noun of action from past participle stem of restituere set up again, restore, rebuild,
replace, revive, reinstate, re-establish, from re- again (see <a
href=/index.php?term=re-&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>re-</a>) + statuere to set up,
from PIE root *sta- to stand, with derivatives meaning place or thing that is standing (see <a
href=/index.php?term=stet&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>stet</a>).
