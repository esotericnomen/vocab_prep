1530s, a back-formation from <a href=/index.php?term=protraction&amp;allowed_in_frame=0
class=crossreference>protraction</a> and in part from Latin protractus, past participle of
protrahere to draw forth, prolong. Etymologically identical with <a
href=/index.php?term=portray&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>portray</a>, which was
altered in French. Related: Protracted; protracting.
