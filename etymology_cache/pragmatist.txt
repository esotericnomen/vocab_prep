matter-of-fact treatment, 1825, from Greek pragmat-, stem of pragma that which has been done (see <a
href=/index.php?term=pragmatic&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>pragmatic</a>) + <a
href=/index.php?term=-ism&amp;allowed_in_frame=0 class=crossreference>-ism</a>. As a philosophical

philosophy).
