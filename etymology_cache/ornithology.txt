ornithology (n.)

1670s, from Modern Latin ornithologia (1590s); see ornitho- + -logy.


ornithological (adj.)

1802, from ornithology + -ical. Related: Ornithologically.


ornithologist (n.)

1670s, from ornithology + -ist.


smutty (adj.)

1590s, soiled with smut (of grain); 1660s, indecent, from smut + -y (2). Related: Smuttily;
smuttiness. Smutty-nosed in ornithology means having black nostrils.
