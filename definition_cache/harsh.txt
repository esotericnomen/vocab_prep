Harsh means severe or strict, particularly when referring to punishment. It would be a harsh
punishment, if you got grounded for two weeks just for coming home a little late for dinner.

There are many shades of meaning for the word harsh depending on its context, but all imply that
something is more unpleasant than it needs to be. Harsh lighting hurts your eyes. A harsh
disciplinarian gives out punishments that are too severe. Whereas constructive criticism is usually
helpful, a harsh critique will only make you upset. The harsh reality of the world today is that
millions of people are starving while others have too much.
