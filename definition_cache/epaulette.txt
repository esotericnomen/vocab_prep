An epaulette is a shoulder piece that's part of many military uniforms. It's a type of colorful
decoration that sometimes signifies rank.

If you look at a military uniform, there are many things you might see, such as stripes and medals.
Another is epaulettes, which are ornamentations found on the shoulders. Epaulettes are usually
colorful, and they often convey a higher rank. You probably wouldn't find epaulettes on a foot
soldier, but you probably would on a general. Epaulettes will vary in size and color depending on
the rank and branch of the military. Epaulette comes from a French word for shoulder.
