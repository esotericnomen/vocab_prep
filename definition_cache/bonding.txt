You're bonding with a person or animal when you develop a close emotional connection with them.
Touching your tongue to a frozen flagpole is also a way of bonding, though this is not recommended.

When the baby hippo in the zoo snuggles up to her mother, they are bonding. And though it may not
seem like it, even sitting for hours in the backseat with your annoying little brother is a way of
bonding, that is, building a relationship through constant contact. Oh dear, are you still bonding
with that flagpole? Here's a tip: pour warm water on your tongue. But don't pull!
