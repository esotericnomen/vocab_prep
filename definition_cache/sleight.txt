The noun sleight refers to being able to use your hands with ease, especially when doing a trick.
Sleight is often used in the phrase sleight of hand. If you are a good magician, you can make a coin
disappear with sleight of hand.

The noun sleight refers to cunning or cleverness, especially when used to trick or deceitfully. You
can use a sleight of mind to trick yourself into believing that if you eat a box of cookies at
dinnertime it counts as dinner. The word sleight with its meaning as being sneaky has a long history
and comes from the Middle English word, sleghth, which also meant cunning. Back then people would
have pronounced the gh even though today we don't.
