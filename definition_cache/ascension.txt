The word ascension describes the act of rising up through the air. When a plane takes off, it is in
ascension, or rising up in the sky.

The noun ascension derives from the Latin word ascendere, meaning to mound, ascend, go up.  You may
have observed the ascension, or rising, of a star above the horizon with your telescope.  Watching
the ascension of a space shuttle up into the earth's atmosphere can be a thrilling experience. The
ascension of a hot air balloon up into the sky can be a beautiful sight on a summer's evening.
