Transport means to move something, often large quantities of commercial products, over great
distances. Container ships transport goods made in China to the U.S., where they will be sold.

A pipeline can transport oil. A school bus transports children. Soldiers get their postings but then
must wait for news of their transport. If you are in a hurry to get something to market, you might
consider using air transport, though that's expensive. Transport is from Latin words meaning carry
across.
