Something effulgent radiates light. On a clear day the sun can be quite effulgent. You might need a
pair of shades.

Effulgent comes from the Latin ex meaning out and fulgere meaning to shine. A light that is
effulgent, shines out or radiates. A personality that is effulgent radiates warmth and goodness.
When you're in love, you have an effulgent or radiant look, as though sunlight were shining from
your eyes.
