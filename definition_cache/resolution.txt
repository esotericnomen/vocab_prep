The noun resolution has a few related meanings having to do with being firmly determined about
something. If you lack determination, you'll never fulfill your New Year's resolutions.

Resolution is the noun form of the verb resolve, which comes from Latin resolvere, to loosen, undo,
settle. We can still see this meaning in resolution, in the sense of an explanation or a solution;
when a problem, conflict or mystery reaches its resolution, it has been undone, so to speak. Another
common meaning is determination, resolve: Jose approached the task with resolution.  A related sense
is a decision to do something: My resolution is to go to the gym three times a week.
