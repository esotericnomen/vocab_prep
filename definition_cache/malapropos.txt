It would be malapropos to wear full clown makeup to your cousin's formal wedding. In other words, it
wouldn't be appropriate.

Use the adjective malapropos to describe something that is awkwardly unsuitable for the situation or
setting at hand. Telling jokes at a funeral or bringing your pet piglet to tea at the Queen's castle
would both be highly malapropos. The word comes from the French phrase mal Ă  propos, literally
badly for the purpose. As it's French, you don't pronounce the final s.
