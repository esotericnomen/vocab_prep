When you are careless or reckless and you don’t take care of a person, object or situation like
you ought to, you are practicing negligence. Her negligence of the house resulted in its being
condemned by the city.

The noun negligence comes from a Latin word that means “carelessness.”  Negligence can come in
many forms: negligence of one's children can lead to foster care, negligence while driving can cause
fatal accidents, negligence of work can lead to the loss of a job, and negligence of nutrition can
cause health problems.  There is culpability in negligence. Negligence is when one neglects to do
things that should be done, and there are usually consequences.
