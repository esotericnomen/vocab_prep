A grouch is a cranky person who complains a lot. You could describe your grumpy old Grandpa as a
grouch.

Use the noun grouch when you're talking about someone who's habitually in a terrible mood. If your
usual bus driver is a grouch, he's probably always yelling at his passengers. You can also use
grouch as a verb, to describe what a grouch does: grumble and gripe. The word grouch was originally
1890s United States college slang that might have come from grutch, to murmur or complain.
