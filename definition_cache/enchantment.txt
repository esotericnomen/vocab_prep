Exams over? School out for the summer? You may be feeling a sense of enchantment, that is, a sense
of delight and escape from everyday reality.

The psychological state of enchantment is so strong it can feel like you are under a spell or a
trance, and these words are often interchangeable with enchantment. A work of art that is
particularly captivating and enjoyable can also be termed an enchantment. The Winnie the Pooh books
are often called enchantments, and their sense of child-like innocence and simplicity is typical of
the sensation of enchantment.
