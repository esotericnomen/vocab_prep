Something that is bleak is gloomy and depressing. If it's raining and dark, you might describe the
night as bleak. If you have looked for work and no one will hire you, you could describe your
prospects as bleak.

If you and the ten people sharing your lifeboat have been adrift for ten days and are down to your
last cracker, your situation is bleak. A near synonym is dismal. Bleak is from Middle English bleik,
from Old Norse bleikr white, pale. This word is related to the English word bleach.
