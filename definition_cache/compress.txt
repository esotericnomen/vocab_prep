The noun compress refers to a pad that's held to your body to reduce pain or symptoms of illness. A
washcloth dipped in cold water and pressed onto your forehead to help you cool down is a type of
compress.

As a verb, compress means to squeeze together with a fair amount of pressure. When you compress a
sponge to get all the water out, you press it between your palms or squish it between your fingers.
