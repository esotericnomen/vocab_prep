Corpulent is a formal word that describes a fat person. If you are trying to be respectful when
describing a fat man, you might refer to him as a corpulent gentleman.

There are certainly many other ways to describe a fat person: obese, overweight, and stout are only
a few. Corpulent is from Latin corpulentus, from corpus body. The Latin suffix -ulentus,
corresponding to English -ulent, has the meaning full of, having in quantity.
