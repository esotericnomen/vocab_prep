As a noun, entrance means an act of entering or something that provides a way to enter something.
You can make a grand entrance when you arrive at a party, as long as you can actually find the
entrance.

Entrance has two pronunciations. If you put the accent on the first syllable, the word is a noun
meaning the act of entering or the way into something (an entrance to the building). If the accent
is on the second syllable, then entrance is a verb meaning to enchant, charm, or enamor
