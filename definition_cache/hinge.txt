A hinge is a type of joint that attaches two things together while allowing for limited movement. A
door hinge fastens the door to the wall and lets the door swing open.

A hinge is a joint that holds two pieces of something together while allowing one piece to move in a
swinging motion. When used as a verb, hinge can mean to attach a hinge. Hinge can also be used to
indicate that one key event depends on another, as in “The athlete’s future with the team will
hinge on his performance at tonight’s game” or “The success of this product hinges on how many
units are sold this month.”
