As a setting, a default is automatic.  You weren't sure why your new TV kept returning to the
factory defaults until you realized you were sitting on the remote.

Default can also refer to a lack of other options. You shop at Green's by default; it's the only
grocery store in town.  Default is also a failure to pay a financial obligation. The number of loan
defaults was down this month. As a verb, it means to fail to pay. Your credit score will go down if
you default on a loan.
