A rubric is a heading or a category in a chart, or a rule of conduct. A teacher's grading rubrics
may include participation, homework completion, tests, quizzes, and papers.

A rubric can also mean a rule or a procedure. If you use might makes right as the rubric for the
formation of a list of classroom rules, you'll have a different-feeling classroom culture than if
your rubric is everyone deserves respect.
